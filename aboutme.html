About Me

I'm an open book

As a young girl in chicago, my parents had me in the car on the weekends going to farms in Indiana. we would pick out the ground my own veggies. We would also visit Howard & Sons to select our meats. I have fond bonding time with my mother,blancing and freezing veggies also butchering and portioning meats.

So naturally I fell in love with food. My aunt and mom taught me how to cook. At the age of 11 I made Thanksgiving dinner for 13 adults. My proudest accomplishment at that age, I wanted to learn everything about food.

In the 90's I graduated from high school and entered the military, the food was horrible. In 2001 my military time ended. I then worked as a resturant manager, once again it rekindled my passion for food.

My sister approched me about attending culinary school and 2 months later we were Miami bound. Johnson & Wales is our alma mater, I then worked for Disney World as a cook. I worked in an open Kitchen, it was great seeing the faces of guest as they ate the food I made. I returned to Miami, once again an became a manager. 2007 we entered a recession. I fled to Seattle, where in learned all about beer & bourbon.

I enjoy cooking and traveling, especially learning the source of diffrent cuisines. Also Im a bartender and brewer, so traveling to understand my carft is such an asset to my passion for this industry.
